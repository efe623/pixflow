Common Questions

How do I get started? +

Just send an email to pixfloww@gmail.com with your idea. Describe what you need and a plan with timeline will be sent back within 24 hours.

Do I need to know anything about tech? +

Not at all. Just describe what you want in plain English. Everything is handled — design, code, and launching the site live.
